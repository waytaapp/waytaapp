Wayta UX Audit · Part 2

The top fixes, redesigned.

Before → after mockups for the five highest-impact findings, rebuilt on the Wayta design system: amber on ink, flat charcoal cards with 1px borders, mono for prices and order codes, pill CTAs, sentence-case copy. No mesh, no pulse, no grid.
